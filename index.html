Les Quatre Cents Coups

One of my personal favourites of the movement is Truffaut's 'Les Quatre Cents Coups'. The film follows a 12 year old boy (Antoine) as he navigates a flurry of troubles. It kicks off when the candle Antoine used for his altar dedicated to Honore de Balzac, and ends with the boy in a youth delinquent centre.

In my opinion the film captures the essence of French culture and social dynamics very effectively. Even more precisely it follows Antoine through the streets of Paris (where I grew up).
